Master Guitar Arpeggios and Scales

Arpeggio Pro is a Progressive Web App (PWA) designed for serious musicians. Visualize major, minor, augmented, and diminished arpeggios, plus jazz extensions (7th, 9th, 11th, 13th).

Explore the Major Scale, Pentatonics, Blues scales, and all seven Greek modes (Ionian, Dorian, Phrygian, Lydian, Mixolydian, Aeolian, Locrian) across the fretboard.

Key Features:

  • Full support for Drop D, Open G, DADGAD, and Standard tunings.
  • Professional metronome with multiple time signatures.
  • Left-handed and right-handed modes.
  • Works offline on iOS and Android.
